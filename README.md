# Privacy Policy
Last Updated April 2026


## 1. Introduction

Welcome to our Caddie Cards. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Store

We do not collect or transmit personal data to any servers.

All data used by the app is stored locally on your device using technologies such as AsyncStorage.

This includes:

Game decks you create and edit
Your acceptance of our Terms and Conditions
Your Premium status (e.g., whether you have unlocked paid features)

This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

#### Purchases

If you make a purchase (such as upgrading to Premium), transactions are processed through app stores (e.g., Apple App Store or Google Play). We do not store your payment information.

## 3. How This Information Is Used

The locally stored data is used solely to:

Save and manage your created decks
Enable app functionality
Remember your Premium access
Track acceptance of Terms and Conditions

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not:

Sell your data
Share your data with third parties
Transmit your data to external servers

Because all data is stored locally, we do not have access to it.

## 5. Data Retention

All data remains on your device until:

You delete it within the app, or
You uninstall the app (which may remove stored data)

## 6. Your Rights and Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

You have full control over your data
You can delete app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Security

Because data is stored only on your device, its security depends on your device’s security settings. We do not transmit or store your data externally.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last Updated” date.
